Besart's Logbook

Week 7

Tasks Completed

Recording Demonstrations

This week I recorded demonstrations of the elevator system to show the current progress of the project. The recordings documented the elevator's operation and demonstrated the successful integration of the hardware and software components.

These demonstrations were also used to prepare for the final showcase by providing clear examples of the system's functionality and testing results.

Supporting Partner Work

I filled in on portions of work for a project partner who was away to ensure that project progress continued without delays. This involved reviewing outstanding tasks, assisting with implementation, and helping maintain development momentum.

Working across multiple project areas also gave me a better understanding of the overall system integration and team workflow.

CAN Signal Recording

I recorded CAN bus communication during elevator operation using an oscilloscope. The captured waveforms documented communication activity between the STM32 controllers while responding to floor requests and elevator movement.

These recordings were added to the project documentation to support testing and verify that the CAN network was operating as expected.

Presentation Design and Showcase Preparation

I contributed to the design and organization of the final project presentation. This included preparing technical content, organizing slides, and ensuring the presentation clearly communicated the project's objectives, implementation, and testing results.

I also helped prepare the demonstration sequence that would be presented during the final project showcase.

Logbook Optimization

I improved the formatting and organization of the project logbooks to create a more consistent appearance across the website. This included refining the layout, improving readability, and ensuring weekly progress was documented clearly.

These updates made the documentation more professional while keeping the weekly development history organized.

Challenges

One challenge this week was balancing project documentation, presentation preparation, exams, and technical testing simultaneously. Careful planning was required to ensure demonstrations, CAN signal recordings, and supporting documentation accurately reflected the current state of the project.

What I Learned

This week I learned the importance of documenting technical demonstrations and recording communication signals to support system validation. I also gained more experience presenting technical work in a clear and organized manner for project showcases.

Next Steps

  • Complete additional system validation and testing.
  • Finalize the Phase-I materials.
  • Continue supporting project integration and website improvements.
